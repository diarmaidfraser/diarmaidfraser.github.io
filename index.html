Who I am

A hardworking, energetic and motivated PGA Professional who has a great love for the game of golf and wishes to pass on his knowledge and enthusiasm for the game to people of all ages and levels of the game. An effective and friendly communicator, who works well with others and enjoys their company. Readily equipped with the necessary skills to coach individual golfers and groups with a high level of understanding and usage of the latest technologies in golf. Motivated to encourage golfers and businesses to be successful in achieving their goals. An outgoing personality with the willingness to travel outside of Ireland in order to grow the game of golf.
